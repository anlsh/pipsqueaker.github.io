Death of the Null Cipher?

The Null Cipher had a few problems, such as bloating the size of messages, but was relatively secure- in fact, there is still research going on today as to how to detect and break null ciphers. The Null Cipher was left behind, with the Vignere Cipher promising quicker encryption.

The Null Cipher has had a resurgence of sorts in modern times, with the field of Noise Encryption drawing heavily on its principles.
