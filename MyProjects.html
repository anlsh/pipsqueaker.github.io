Over the years I've taken on quite a few projects to pass the time and teach myself new skills. You can find some of them on my github.

Here are a few examples of my work.

  • Pac-Man!
    For my AP Computer Science project, I made an almost-faithful clone of Pac-Man in Python. For the graphics I used raw OpenGL, as I working with low-level graphics is one of my hobbies.
  • PlaylistSplitter
    During High School, I was very active on my FRC team. One of the problems that we faced was that it was a very time-consuming to upload match videos to TheBlueAlliance, a community hub. To solve this, I wrote a script in Python to automatically deduce the match id from a video's title and submit it to the corresponding page on TBA.
